History of the temple

Swamiye Saranam Iyappa The temple has been constructed by a Trust called Sree Bhoothanatha Swamy Public Charitable Trust. It is mission of the trust to adopt, establish, develop, renovate and manage abandoned temples across Tamil Nadu and The Lord ayyappa came in the dream of Sri Padmanabha Guruswamy, who was the Author of the trust and ordered him to build a Temple for him. The temple was started during the year 2008 and was stalled during the year 2011. Later after Covid 19 in the year 2020, the new set of trustees along with two of the old trustees has taken steps to complete the construction and with the blessings of Lord ayyappan and the support and generous donation of all the Aasthegas, the temple construction is complete and the Kumbabhishegam was decided to be performed on Sunday June 25th 2023.Mr.S.Sathyanarayanan of S&S foundation has been very supportive to his project by offering the land at a very discounted price.
